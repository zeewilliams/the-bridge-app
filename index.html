Our Story

My grandmother wanted to set up a time once a week to learn about the Bible together as a family. So I took it upon myself to set up the study sessions and host them.

As time went on, I took the notes and feedback I received from my family during those sessions — the questions, the confusion, the moments things finally clicked — and used them to make the Bible clearer and provide a deeper understanding.

Over time, this is what I created to help us learn the Bible together. And now I want to share it with the world — so others can start learning about Jesus with their families too, and spread the Gospel with confidence.

Core Principle
"Scripture Interprets Scripture"
Every explanation in this app is grounded in other Bible verses — not human opinion or modern reinterpretation. We let God's Word explain itself. The AI is instructed to follow this principle on every response.
Privacy Promise
Your data never leaves your device. No accounts. No tracking. No ads. No servers storing your information. Your progress, notes, and insights live only on your phone. Your AI key is stored only on your device and sent only to Anthropic.
