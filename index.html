Our Story

My grandmother wanted to set up a time once a week to learn about the Bible together as a family. So I took it upon myself to set up the study sessions and host them.

As time went on, I took the notes and feedback I received from my family during those sessions — the questions, the confusion, the moments things finally clicked — and used them to make the Bible clearer and provide a deeper understanding.

Core Principle
"Scripture Interprets Scripture"
The Bible is its own best commentary. Every explanation in this app is grounded in other Bible verses — not human opinion or modern reinterpretation. We let God's Word explain itself.
